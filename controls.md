# Canon EOS R5 Owner-Confirmed Controls

**Status:** Current owner-confirmed setup as of 2026-07-23.

The earlier custom-control screenshots are retired historical material. The camera settings changed after those images were made, so the images are not reliable evidence of the current setup and must not be used to reconstruct or contradict this table.

## Evidence Boundaries

- **Canon capability:** a function the camera supports. Capability alone does not establish the chosen setup.
- **Owner-confirmed setting:** the authoritative current assignment shown below.
- **Recommendation:** advice, always labeled as such; it is not a current setting until the owner confirms it.
- **Unresolved:** no assignment may be inferred or documented as settled.
