Checklist

  • Servo AF
  • Bird/eye acquired
  • Shutter speed verified
  • Histogram checked
  • Focus limiter set for distance

Watch For

  • Motion blur
  • Tracking the wrong bird
  • Bright-sky underexposure
  • Buffer filling

Common Mistakes

  • Shutter too slow
  • Failing to adjust exposure for the background
  • Staying with subject detection when it repeatedly selects the wrong target
  • Poor panning

Notes

  • Use Expand AF Area when the bird is small or obscured, the background is busy, or Face + Tracking repeatedly selects the wrong target. Subject to Detect does not apply to Expand AF Area on the original EOS R5.
  • Use Image Stabilization Mode 3 for erratic flight and action.
  • Switch to Mode 2 only for deliberate steady panning.
