Checklist

  • RGB Histogram on
  • Highlight Alert enabled
  • Continuous AF off
  • Electronic full-time MF off
  • IBIS High Resolution Shot off

Watch For

  • Noise reduction accidentally enabled
  • JPEG-only specialty modes left on
  • Lens-specific focus options not available

Common Mistakes

  • Treating setup items like field profile choices
  • Leaving a specialty mode enabled after testing

Notes

  • Highlight tools stay on because exposure warnings are useful in the field.
  • High ISO Noise Reduction stays off because Lightroom/Topaz handles RAW cleanup better.
  • Long Exposure NR can reduce hot pixels and fixed-pattern noise in selected multi-second exposures, but its additional dark-frame wait interrupts shooting; leave it off when cadence matters.
  • Continuous AF is off because it can drain battery by focusing when you are not shooting.
  • Electronic full-time MF is off because it is not useful for your lens.
  • IBIS High Resolution Shot is off because it is a static-scene specialty mode with very large JPEG output.
