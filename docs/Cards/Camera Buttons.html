Checklist

  • Practice back-button AF before a critical shoot.
  • Configure and test the registered AF function.
  • Confirm Manual + Auto ISO before using exposure compensation.
  • Recheck assignments after a reset or firmware update.

Watch For

  • Registered AF behavior not configured or tested
  • Exposure Compensation on Control Ring requires Auto ISO in Manual exposure

Common Mistakes

  • Expecting shutter half-press to autofocus after enabling back-button AF
  • Treating recommended assignments as confirmation of the camera's current settings
